UP IN THE CLOUDS

They are a beautiful sight, those cumulus clouds drifting by slowly high above our heads. But what is propelling them? And how do they affect the wind? To find out Louise Nuijens spent a few days with her head in the clouds. Literally. A story about the CloudBrake Flight Campaign in June 2019 for TU Delft's Stories of Science.
